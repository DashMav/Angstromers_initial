Dear Students,

We are thrilled to announce an exceptional internship opportunity that promises to shape your future in the field of technology! We are excited to extend an invitation to participate in our groundbreaking project aimed at developing and nurturing a highly skilled workforce capable of managing projects across diverse domains.

Our project emphasizes a hands-on training approach, providing invaluable experience through participation in real-world projects under the mentorship of industry experts from Angstormers.   2. Internet of Things (IoT)
  3. 5G/6G Technologies
  4. Advanced Encryption Standard (AES)
  5. Power Electronics/Electric Vehicles (PE/EV)
  6. Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI)
  7. Cybersecurity
